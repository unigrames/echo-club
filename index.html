Welcome to Eco Club

Discover the beauty and benefits of plants.

The Eco Club at MIMS is a vibrant community dedicated to promoting environmental awareness and sustainability. Our mission is to inspire and educate individuals about the importance of preserving nature and adopting eco-friendly practices. Through various initiatives, workshops, and programs, we aim to create a greener and healthier planet. The club organizes tree plantation drives, waste management campaigns, and educational sessions on topics like renewable energy, biodiversity, and conservation. Join us in our journey to make a positive impact on the environment and foster a culture of sustainability.
